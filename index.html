About Maxtron Associates

Established in 2017, Maxtron Associates started as a transportation service provider to one of the leading operators of Common Biomedical Waste Disposal and Treatment Facilities of Kerala. We took responsibility sharing with the parent company, when bio medical waste management in Kerala was a challenge. Be it individual homes or apartments, healthcare, government, transportation, commercial, institutional and industrial sectors; you can depend on MAXTRON ASSOCIATES for customized waste management related supplies that deliver bottom-line results.
